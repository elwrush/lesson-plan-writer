Finding details

Now let's learn a strategy for multiple-choice questions.

Then you'll listen again and choose the best answers.

Multiple Choice Strategy

"Who do teenagers listen to most for advice, according to the presenter?"

Step 1: Read the question carefully

What is this question asking? Who? About what?

It wants to know who teenagers go to most for advice.

Step 2: Underline key words

"Who do teenagers listen to most for advice?"

These words tell you exactly what to listen for.

Step 3: Read the options before you listen

  • a) pop stars
  • b) their parents
  • c) YouTubers

Think: What do I already know? Which one is most likely?

Now listen for the answer.

Step 4: Confirm your answer

"Who do teenagers listen to most for advice?"

The presenter says: "In fact, the people we listen to most are YouTubers!"

The answer is c — YouTubers. ✓

Listen and Choose

  • Read questions 1-5 on page 11 (Task 4). Underline key words.
  • Read the options a-c for each question.
  • Listen to the podcast again (1.02) and choose the best answer (Task 5).
