What did you do during your holiday?

Display the questions. Give students a moment to think. Tell them they will share their ideas in pairs first, then with the class. This is a Think-Pair-Share activity.

Share your ideas

First, think quietly by yourself.

Then, share with a partner.

Finally, tell the class what your partner said!

Think — Pair — Share

  1. Think (3 min) — Write down what you did and one goal for this term.
  2. Pair (7 min) — Tell your partner. Listen carefully.
  3. Share (8 min) — Tell the class about your partner's goal.
